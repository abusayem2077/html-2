Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence or better known as "AI", is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision. AI programming focuses on three cognitive skills: learning, reasoning and self-correction.
